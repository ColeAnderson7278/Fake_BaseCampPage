About Us

Base Camp is a program located in WaterValley, M.S. that is partnered with several businesses to teach a select few students the basics of working in the programming field. It was started to help fill in the many job opening gaps that existed in the rural working environment of Mississippi and to do so in a short period of time.

Getting In

To get into the Base Camp program you must first be nominated by usually a teacher or mentor of some sort. After this you will be interviewed and questioned to see if you are a good fit for this program. Only then can you become a Base Camp student.

The Goal

The final goal of Base Camp is not to make the students experts, but rather to make sure they are knowledgeable enough to get their foot in the door and build on what they are taught. This will then help lead them to the final goal which is getting a job at the end of the 11 months.
